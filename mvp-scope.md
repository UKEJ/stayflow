# StayFlow MVP Scope

## Goal

Build a production-ready Hospitality Operating System for small and medium-sized hospitality businesses.

---

# Included in Version 1.0

## Authentication

- Register
- Login
- Logout
- Forgot Password
- Email Verification

---

## Business Management

- Create Business
- Update Business
- Invite Team Members
- Manage Roles

---

## Property Management

- Create Property
- Update Property
- Property Settings

---

## Unit Management

- Unit Types
- Individual Units
- Pricing
- Availability

---

## Guest Management

- Guest Profiles
- Guest History

---

## Reservations

- Create Reservation
- Modify Reservation
- Cancel Reservation
- Check-in
- Check-out

---

## Payments

- Record Payments
- Payment History
- Invoice Generation

---

## Dashboard

- Occupancy
- Revenue
- Reservations
- Recent Activity

---

## Notifications

- Email Notifications
- In-App Notifications

- AI Features
- Marketplace
- Academy
- Hiring
- Restaurant & POS
- Laundry
- Inventory
- CRM
- Marketing Automation
- Mobile App
- Third-party Integrations

---

# Success Criteria

- A business can register.
- A business can create one or more properties.
- Staff can be invited.
- Units can be added.
- Guests can make reservations.
- Payments can be recorded.
- Users can manage daily operations from one dashboard.
